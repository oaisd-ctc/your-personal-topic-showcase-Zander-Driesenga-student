The History

The concept for the game was originated when Digital Extremes made a game called Dark Sector

The Gameplay

The main gameplay of Warframe is very fast paced with a lot of different movment options which include a slide, double jump, and bullet jump

You play as "robot" called a Warframe which has four unique abilities that cost energy

You also are able to make and use over 500 different weapons to kill the enemy

Warframe is a very grindy game that can sometimes take a while to get what you want and a lot of the time you might need the wiki to look at to know where to go to get thing you need
