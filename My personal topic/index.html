Warframe is a game that does a lot

Warframe is a free to play, co-op, third-person, looter shooter with thousands of hours of content

It is developed by Digital Extremes (DE) and was first released on March 2013 and has been reciving constant free expansions to this day

The game has a lot of weapons, warframes, missions, quests, and activities that for some newer players it might get overwhelmed by everything that there is to do
